Google Play Movies &amp; TV dishing out free digital copy of X-Men movie to select subscribers (update)

July 2022 · 1 minute read

Google appears to be feeling generous yet again. Last week, the search giant treated a few of its subscribers to a free digital copy of Sandra Bullock’s critically acclaimed Gravity and now the company is continuing its push of complimentary films with free access to Fox’s X-Men.

We fired up our Nexus Player to check out the media hub’s latest software and noticed that a free copy of the popular comic book flick was available to add to our collection as a “gift from Google.” We’ve contacted Google for comment on this promo and we’ll update things here once we get more clarity on how this offer works, but in the meantime you might want to check your collection at Google Play Movies & TV.

Update: Google has informed us that this offer is available to all “living room” devices, which includes the Nexus Player, Chromecast and Roku.
